Sponsorships & Partnerships

The Kogi International Carnival offers a unique platform for brands to engage with a diverse audience, enhance their visibility, and align with a national event that celebrates culture, unity, and development. Our sponsorship packages provide a range of benefits that allow your brand to be part of an extraordinary cultural celebration.

Why Sponsor the Kogi International Carnival?

As a sponsor of the Kogi International Carnival, your brand will gain unprecedented visibility, aligning itself with a national event that celebrates unity, culture, and development. Sponsorship packages offer a range of tailored benefits designed to maximize brand exposure before, during, and after the carnival. Below are the key benefits and justifications

Massive Brand Visibility

With over 200,000 vehicles transiting Kogi State daily, your brand will be featured prominently on 500 billboards, street poles, and high-rise buildings strategically placed across the state. Sponsors will benefit from extended exposure in the months leading up to the carnival and for three months after the event. This means sustained brand awareness and recall across a broad demographic. Fliers and Stickers will carry same on vehicles across the state as well as those transiting the start.

Social Media Engagement

Sponsors will enjoy extensive digital promotion across the carnival’s official websites, social media platforms, and affiliated channels, ensuring continuous brand exposure. The campaign will run from the launch date through to the first quarter of the following year, maximizing reach and engagement with a tech-savvy audience.

Association with Culture and Community

The carnival will foster an emotional connection between your brand and the audience through association with Kogi’s rich cultural heritage and community engagement. Sponsoring an event that celebrates unity, tradition, and national pride will position your brand as a champion of cultural preservation and community development.

On-Ground Activation

With 10,000 youths, 2,000 performers, and 1,000 children actively engaged in the event, sponsors have the opportunity to activate on-ground brand interactions. This will include product placements, booths in carnival villages, merchandise distribution, and sampling, providing a direct link to potential consumers in an immersive setting.

Access to VIP Networks

Sponsors will have the unique opportunity to connect with top dignitaries, political leaders, traditional rulers, and influential figures from across Nigeria. The carnival will attract a high-profile audience, including decision-makers and business leaders, allowing sponsors to network, build relationships, and explore new opportunities.

Association with Culture and Community

Sponsors will be featured in 1,000 exclusive copies of the Kogi Coffee Table Book, a premium souvenir that will be distributed to top dignitaries and hospitality outfits nationwide. This ensures that your brand remains front of mind long after the carnival, with the book being displayed in prestigious locations.

Reinforcing Brand Image

Kogi State’s strategic location as a transit hub for 36 states (19 in the North, 17 in the South) provides a unique marketing advantage. Sponsoring the carnival aligns your brand with Kogi’s growing role as a tourist and economic center. Tens of thousands of people travel through the state each day, ensuring constant brand interaction.

Exclusive VIP Access and Tickets

Sponsors will receive VIP passes to the carnival’s major events, offering unparalleled access to exclusive areas within the carnival cities and arenas. These passes will provide a premium experience, further enhancing your brand’s association with prestige and exclusivity.

Long-Term Media Exposure

Through continuous media coverage, including print, radio, television, and online platforms, sponsors will receive long-term exposure. All media content will feature sponsors prominently and ensure that brand visibility is maximized across local, national, and international platforms.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Supporting the carnival demonstrates your brand’s commitment to community development, youth engagement, and cultural preservation. By participating in this national celebration, your brand will be recognized for its role in uplifting local economies, creating job opportunities, and supporting Nigeria’s cultural heritage.

Sponsorship Tiers

The Kogi International Carnival offers a unique opportunity for sponsors to elevate their brand visibility, engage with a diverse audience, and align with one of Nigeria’s most culturally rich events. With several sponsorship tiers available, brands can choose the level of exposure and engagement that best suits their marketing and corporate goals.

Platinum Sponsor

300 MILLION

As the premier tier, Platinum Sponsors will enjoy exclusive access to all the benefits of the Kogi International Carnival, ensuring maximum visibility across all platforms and activities. :

  • Branding on 200 billboards, 500 street poles, and high-rise building decorations throughout the state, offering unmatched outdoor exposure.
  • Feature in the exclusive Coffee Table Book distributed to top dignitaries, luxury outlets, and hospitality venues nationwide, giving your brand long-term visibility.
  • Top-tier digital presence across all online platforms, including websites, social media, and event publications, ensuring wide-spread engagement.
  • VIP Access for top executives, including access to a dedicated sponsor lounge and networking opportunities with key dignitaries and decision-makers.
  • Full engagement in event-related marketing and a customized sponsor activation booth within the carnival villages.

Gold Sponsor

200 MILLION

Gold Sponsors will receive 75% of the benefits offered to Platinum sponsors, making it a highly visible option with significant perks:

  • Branding on 100 billboards and 250 street poles across key locations in Kogi State.
  • Featured mention in all digital promotions and printed materials, boosting brand recognition.
  • VIP tickets to the carnival’s grand opening and closing events, offering access to the most exclusive ceremonies and opportunities to connect with influential figures.
  • Prominent placement in all media publications, including websites and social media.

Silver Sponsor

150 MILLION

Silver Sponsors will receive 50% of the top-tier benefits, striking a balance between brand exposure and budget:

  • Brand placement on 50 billboards and major streets, ensuring visibility across critical areas.
  • Full coverage on all online platforms and carnival materials.
  • Access to product booths for sampling during carnival events, providing an opportunity for direct consumer engagement.
  • General access tickets to key events during the carnival.

Bronze Sponsor

75 MILLION

The Bronze Sponsor tier offers 25% of the exclusive benefits, making it ideal for brands seeking visibility within a more affordable budget:

  • Brand placement on 25 billboards and key carnival routes.
  • Limited digital promotions and mentions in select event materials.
  • Access to product placement booths in select carnival villages.
  • General access tickets to select carnival events.

Basic Sponsor

20 MILLION

This entry-level sponsorship provides essential brand exposure at the event and online:

  • Brand visibility on smaller promotional materials and online platforms.
  • Inclusion in media promotions throughout the event period.
  • Access to carnival materials and digital features on official event websites and social media channels.
